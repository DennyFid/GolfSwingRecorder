Goal
Create an Android application that automatically records a golf swing without requiring the golfer to touch the phone after setup.

The phone will:

lean against a tee marker

approximately six feet from and in front of  the golfer

record landscape video

automatically begin recording when the golfer starts the swing

automatically stop after the follow-through

save the clip

immediately be ready for the next swing

The app should work completely offline.
